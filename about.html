About Me

My name is Stanislav Pinchuk, but I go by Stan. I was orignially born in Kyrgyzstan. My family and I moved to the USA in 1996 when I was 5 years of age. I grew up in State College, PA and moved to Charlotte, NC in 2005.

I am a Full Stack Developer who got interested in the IT field a few years after I graduated High School. I started working in Networking but had always wanted to study programming. I completed a Full Stack Development Bootcamp through the University of North Carolina at Charlotte.

I have a strong desire to learn new skills and expand my knowledge. I keep myself busy pursuing new challenges. As the saying goes "Lazy people want much but get little, but those who work hard will prosper."
